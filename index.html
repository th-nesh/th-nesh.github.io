About

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Stuttgart, advised by Prof. Dr. Alina Roitberg, and a member of the International Max Planck Research School for Intelligent Systems (IMPRS-IS). My research focuses on parameter-efficient adaptation of frozen vision foundation models — such as DINOv2 and CLIP — for fine-grained video understanding, favoring lightweight and often training-free methods over full fine-tuning.
